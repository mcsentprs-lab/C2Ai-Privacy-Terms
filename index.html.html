Privacy Policy
Missed Call Text Back Service

Effective Date: March 18, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 18, 2026
This Privacy Policy describes how C2Ai Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with our Missed Call Text Back Service. By using this service, you agree to the practices described below.

01 What This Service Does

Our Missed Call Text Back Service automatically sends an SMS message to individuals who call a business phone number and do not reach a live person. The purpose is to acknowledge the missed call and facilitate a follow-up conversation on behalf of the business.

02 Information We Collect

When you are contacted through this service — or when you interact with it — we may collect the following:

  • Your phone number (automatically captured at the time of the missed call)
  • The date, time, and duration of the missed call
  • Any SMS replies you send in response to automated messages
  • Device and carrier metadata associated with the SMS exchange

We do not collect financial information, passwords, government ID numbers, or other sensitive personal data through this service.

03 How We Use Your Information

Information collected through this service is used exclusively to:

  • Send an automated follow-up text message on behalf of the business you called
  • Facilitate a conversation between you and that business
  • Monitor and improve service performance and deliverability
  • Comply with applicable laws and telecommunications regulations

We do not use your phone number or message content for advertising, marketing to you on behalf of C2Ai Solutions, or any purpose unrelated to the service described above.

04 Third-Party Service Providers

To deliver this service, we transmit certain data to the following third-party provider:

Third Party — SMS Delivery

Twilio, Inc.

We use Twilio to send and receive SMS messages. When a missed call is detected, your phone number and relevant call data are transmitted to Twilio's platform to trigger the outbound text message. Twilio may log message metadata including phone numbers, timestamps, and delivery status.

Twilio's privacy practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy, which can be reviewed at twilio.com/en-us/legal/privacy.

We do not sell your data to Twilio or any other party. Data is shared solely to the extent necessary to operate the service.

05 Data Retention

Call and message data is retained for a limited period consistent with the operational needs of the service and applicable law. Businesses using our platform may have their own data retention policies that govern how long your information is stored within their systems.

If you would like your data removed, see Section 7 below.

06 Opt-Out / Stop

You may opt out of receiving automated text messages from this service at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. After opting out, you will not receive further automated messages unless you re-initiate contact.

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

07 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent to receive automated messages

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the information provided in Section 9.

08 Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information transmitted through this service. However, no SMS-based communication system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection of data in transit.
